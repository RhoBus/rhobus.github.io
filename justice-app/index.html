Justice app

Justice app is a free application about Bolivian legal documents with academic purposes.

This project contains texts about procedural law, laws, codes, the political constitution of the state and also a complete legal dictionary.

Download on Play Store

Brief look at the problem

Bolivian law student usually have to strugle to keep up with the ever-changing legal landscape. They have to lug around heavy textbooks and searching through countless documents for the information they need.

How was this problem solved

All kind of texts related to procedural law, laws, codes have been collected to be searched in the app so users only need to enter the number of article or even a word they can remember.

The app has a searcher implemented so it's easy to use and also a copy function, the user just need to tap on the article and voil√†!
