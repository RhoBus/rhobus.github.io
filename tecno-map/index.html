Tecno Map

Tecno Map is a free application aimed at students of the "Universidad Mayor de San Simón".

Here you can find classrooms, offices and buildings. You only need a stable internet connection and you can access the maps very easily.

Download on Play Store Read the case study Check the Figma design

Brief look at the problem

When newbies are coming to "Universidad Mayor de San Simón", they have to go each class on time but the campus is too large. Even old students have problems to find a classroom, office, or building.

There are printed maps available at the campus but sometimes people don't have the time to check it out, and some others don't know how to read it.

How was this problem solved

All these important locations have been collected to be searched in the app so users need no more but writing the place they want to go.

Of course, a map framework was used. People with smartphones are commonly used to use a map app as Google Maps or even games as Pokémon Go.

Let's talk about design

Less is more, so that's why the design could be felt as "simple" but the idea is to keep it simple so any use would exactly what to do without a tutorial.
